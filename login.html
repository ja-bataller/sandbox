About

Sandbox Games Store is a privately owned retail store by SGC (Sandbox Group Corporation), pioneering in the Philippines. Sandbox promotes international and locally-made technologies and is one of the best retailers of games, computer peripherals, gaming chairs, accessories, and merch's - both local and company brands.
Whether it be console, games, or devices, we are always sure to have the latest and in-demand ones available in our store at a reasonable price tag.
